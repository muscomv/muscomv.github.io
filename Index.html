The elderly Generation

A major problem facing many developing countries, is their aging population. Although retired, the elder generation still want to be productive citizens and contribute to society. Senior citizens have a wealth of wisdom and experience that may benefit younger people. However, being elderly, they may have medical or mobility problems that may hinder their ability to work full time. Senior citizens who live alone sometimes require help with services such as shopping, meals, personal care, healthcare, transportation and home cleaning. Sometimes they would just like to have a companion for a short while, or be able to visit a centre where they can interact with other seniors or take part in group activities.
